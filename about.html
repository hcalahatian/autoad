The Company

AutoAd Trading is a privately owned company established in January 2009, known for supplying acrylic sheets and other advertising materials such as LED lights, power supply, sintra boards, polycarbonate, etc. AutoAd Trading is located in Sampaloc Manila, operates, and delivers products to clients around Metro Manila and nearby provinces of Luzon.

We believe in quality products at a good price and outstanding service will establish trust, confidence and respect with our clients. Our friendly staffs are all experienced and knowledgeable on the different specifications of every material that we have.

Mission

To provide top quality advertising materials at a reasonable price and services that exceeds the expectation of our current and future clients.

Vision

To become the most preferred advertising supplier company in the Philippines when it comes in top quality, cost efficient and environment friendly materials.
